What is BIM

BIM stands for Bachelor of Information Management. It is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on providing students with a comprehensive understanding of information technology, business, and management. The program is designed to equip students with the skills to handle both the technical and business aspects of information systems and management.


Key areas covered in BIM include:


  • Information Technology (IT): Fundamentals of computer systems, networking, and software applications.
  • Business Management: Basics of business operations, organizational behavior, marketing, and finance.
  • Data Management: Understanding how to manage, analyze, and use data effectively for business decisions.
  • Project Management: Techniques to manage and lead IT projects within an organization.
  • Software Development: Learning programming and software design for various business applications.
  • ybersecurity: Ensuring information systems and data are protected from cyber threats.
