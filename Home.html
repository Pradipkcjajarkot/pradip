Undergraduate Programs


Surkhet Model College may offer a range of bachelor's degree programs in various fields. Some of the common programs that are typically available in Surkhet Model colleges include:


    Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS):

  • This is a 4-year program focused on business management, economics, accounting, and finance.
  • Core subjects may include Management, Accounting, Business Communication, Marketing, and Finance.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):


  • A comprehensive program designed to equip students with skills in business management, leadership, and decision-making.
  • Subjects may include Management Principles, Human Resource Management, Organizational Behavior, Finance, Marketing, and Entrepreneurship.

    Bachelor of Information Management (BIM):

  • The BIM program generally takes 4 years to complete, divided into 8 semesters.
  • A program that combines information technology with management studies.
  • Subjects include Database Management Systems, Networking, Software Engineering, Business Management, IT Project Management, and Information Security,and even study.

Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM):


  • A program focused on the hospitality industry, including hotel management, tourism, and catering.
  • Subjects may include Hospitality Operations, Tourism Management, Food Production, and Event Planning.

Postgraduate Programs


Mastar of Business Study

MBS stands for Master of Business Studies. It is a postgraduate degree in business management, typically offered by universities in Nepal and many other countries. The program is designed to provide advanced knowledge and skills in various aspects of business, management, and leadership.

  • The MBS program generally takes 2 years to complete, divided into 4 semesters.
  • The primary goal of the MBS program is to prepare students for leadership and managerial roles in business organizations.
  • It focuses on enhancing analytical skills, decision-making abilities, and business strategy knowledge.
